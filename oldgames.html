Old Games

This was from many many years ago from when I was first learning JavaScript.
I came across them and figured why not share them with the world. All the artwork and programming I did on the first three, and I did almost all of the artwork on the fourth and assisted a friend with the programming.
You need to change your developer tools to run in internet explorer 7 for them to work.

Ninja Cow Tower Defense

A basic tower defense game, make sure you resize your browser properly or run in like Internet Explorer 7.

America Bound

A knockoff version of Oregon Trail that I made, where you are pilgrims on a ship heading for America.

Stick Fighter

Mortal Kombat knockoff where two stick fighters go at it, learning the intricacies of setTimeout in Javascript

Battle Simulator

A DnD knockoff where you battle monsters and get treasure, go to armory, the tavern, etc.
